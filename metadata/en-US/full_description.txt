- FEATURES -

• Cards are sorted by series with the inclusion of promo cards
• An option to hide or gray out cards you already have in your collection
• Cards you are searching for are kept visible in color or at the top of the list
• No ads
• Updated as new series are released
• Works offline
• Simple to use
